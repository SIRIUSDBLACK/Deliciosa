A Slice of Paradise:
Deliciosa

Step into a world where the aroma of freshly baked
dough mingles with the laughter of friends and family.
Welcome to Deliciosa,where every moment is a
celebration of delectable flavors and warm hospitality.

Location

Our restaurant is located in the charming town
of Ciudad Encantada,where cobbled streets,
whitewashed walls,and vibrant bougainvillea
create an atmosphere that's as inviting as it is
unforgettable.Overlooking the shimmering
Mediterranean waters,our terrace provides
the perfect backdrop for enjoying traditional
Spanish dishes with a modern twist.

Chef Michel

Meet Chef Michel, a culinary virtuoso who seamlessly
blends his remarkable talent with his entrepreneurial
spirit. From crafting culinary wonders to owning and
curating Daliciosa, his journey is a testament to passion,
innovation, and a deep love for the art of food.
